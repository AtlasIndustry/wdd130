Tips for Improved Accuracy

Stance: Ensure a stable foundation by standing with your feet shoulder-width apart and knees slightly bent.
Grip: Maintain a firm but relaxed grip on the firearm, focusing on consistent pressure with your dominant hand.
Sight Alignment: Align your front and rear sights, keeping the target in clear focus. Remember, focus on the front sight.
